    «ↀ» is the debut mini album by LOVElution. It was released on August 17, 2023.

    The day of the tripleS’ newest Dimension (unit) LOVElution’s release is bright.

    The name of the album is said to be an expression of «Infinite» or «Infinity» (∞) by rotating the number of members in group; «8», to 90 degrees. tripleS plan to showcase an infinite number of possibilities with this album.

    This album includes the title track «Girl's Capitalism», intro «ↀ», «Complexity», «Black Soul Dress», «Seoul Sonyo Sound», «Cry Baby», «Speed Love», and «Number 8». Title track 'Girls Capitalism' is a song that blends refreshing rhythms and dazzling melodies, and includes many catchy motifs.
