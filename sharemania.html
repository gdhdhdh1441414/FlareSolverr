    "Turn It Up" is a song performed by Dutch DJ and record producer Armin van Buuren. It was released on 15 March 2019 by the label Armind on Armada Music, as the ninth single from the album Balance. The song sounds similar with the 2018 Armin van Buuren's previous song "Blah Blah Blah".

    Armin declared about the song: "I'm always excited for the festival season because of the crowd's response to tracks like these. Nothing beats the feeling you get when fans go absolutely nuts to something you play, and 'Turn It Up' was made for that exact purpose. I'm really looking forward to playing this one all throughout the summer."

    The music video for "Turn It Up" is created by Zinema Productions. The images that can be seen in the video clip were shot during the festival 'A State Of Trance'. The animations in the clip are designed based on the accompanying artwork. In May 2023, the Official Lyric Video of "Turn It Up" has reached 90 million views on YouTube.

    The videotrack is downloaded from Vimeo (Zinema Production) and converterd to HEVC (H.265).
    The PCM track is lossless (Juno Download, CD quality), synced and edited with Adobe Audition.

    "Blah Blah Blah" is a song performed by Dutch DJ and record producer Armin van Buuren. It was released on 18 May 2018 by the label Armada Music and Armind, as the first single from the EP of the same name and the fifth single from his seventh studio album Balance. The song is an unlockable in Fuser (a rhythm game developed by Harmonix).
    The track was written by van Buuren, British singer and songwriter BullySongs (Andrew Bullimore), and Josh Record. The vocals were performed by Bullimore's younger son Aidan.

    Armin declared about the song: "I wouldn't have dared a record like this maybe five years ago. In the meantime I'm fine with fun, crazy ideas. 'Blah Blah Blah' came from an idea of my daughter. She came home with all the dirty words she picked up in the schoolyard, and made a song out of it. I had to do something with that!". The song became a huge club hit worldwide.

    The music video for "Blah Blah Blah" is created by Zinema Productions. The images that can be seen in the video clip were shot during the festival 'A State Of Trance'. In May 2023, the Official Lyric Video of "Blah Blah Blah" has reached 669.9 million views on YouTube.
