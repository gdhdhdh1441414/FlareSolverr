    Realiez (stylized in all-caps) is the fourth mini album by Kang Daniel. It was released on June 19, 2023 with "SOS" serving as the album's title track.

    The pre-release single "Wasteland" was released on June 7, 2023.

    The physical album comes in three versions: A, B and Platform.

    Track list

    "SOS" - 3:11
