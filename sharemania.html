    About This Series
    One thousand years from now, Superman, the legendary Man of Steel, will inspire a group of emerging superheroes of the 31st Century to unite to defend the rights of all free worlds and uphold the laws of the United Planets. That is, if they don't kill each other first. 'Legion of Super-Heroes' is an animated series featuring a young Superman that combines humor with high heroism and high stakes to create the ultimate sci-fi, superhero, wish-fulfillment fantasy for kids of all ages. In each episode, Superman and the Legion face threats and adversaries from other worlds who challenge the team as superheroes and emotionally, immersing audiences in both the interpersonal dramas of the characters and the intergalactic challenges.
