    Unlock My World is the first full-length album by fromis_9. It was released on June 5, 2023 with "#Menow" serving as the album's title track.



    This album marks their first release as an eight member group following the departure of Jang Gyuri on July 31, 2022.[1]

    The physical album comes in fifteen versions: CD (#reality, #imagine, #notyet), KiT (#imagine & #notyet), Weverse Albums, and individual compact versions.



    Track list
    1. "Attitude" - 3:10
    2. "#Menow" - 2:49
    3. "Wishlist" (Jiwon & Chaeyoung) - 2:56
    4. "In The Mirror" (Seoyeon & Nagyung) - 3:31
    5. "Don't Care" (Jiwon, Jisun, Seoyeon, Nagyung) - 3:22
    6. "Prom Night" (Saerom, Hayoung, Chaeyoung, Jiheon) - 3:26
    7. "Bring It On" - 2:51
    8. "What I Want" - 3:29
    9. "My Night Routine" - 2:57
    10. "Eye Contact (눈맞춤)" - 3:30
