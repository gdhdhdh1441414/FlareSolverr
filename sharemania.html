    Hello!!

    In 2011, due to Tōhoku earthquake & Tsunami, many artists united to make compilations from which sales would proceed to go to different entities that would help victims and survivors of both disasters...

    Now none of those compilations are available for purchase, "Pray For Japan Vol 2" was available through iTunes ( https://music.apple.com/us/album/pray-for-japan-with-music-vol-2/430267995 ), while "Recorded for Japan" was available in its' own spinshop site ( http://www.spinshop.com/store/recordedforjapan ).

    What's special about these? Most of the songs in both compilations were exclusive to them, or were unique edits/versions/takes/demos of songs already (or to be) released.

    If anyone still has them in their library, please do share them...!
