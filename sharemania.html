    Seoul Dreaming is the second mini album by H1-KEY. It was released on August 30, 2023 with "Seoul (Such a Beautiful City)" and "Time to Shine" serving as the album's double title tracks.

    "Time to Shine" was pre-released on August 23.



    Track list
    "Intro : Seoul Dreaming" - 1:13
    "Seoul (Such a Beautiful City)" - 3:09
    "Time to Shine (불빛을 꺼뜨리지 마)" - 3:18
    "Low Key Scared But H1-KEY Ready" - 2:54
    "Magical Dream" - 2:55
    "Seoul (Such a Beautiful City) (Inst.)" - 3:09
    "Time to Shine (불빛을 꺼뜨리지 마) (Inst.)" - 3:18
