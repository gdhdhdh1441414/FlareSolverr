    Hidden Content:
    You must either reply or click 'Like' to see the hidden information contained here.
    These are the cleanest acapellas that you'll probably ever get unless the raw stems are leaked. I took each song and inverted it on top of their corresponding instrumentals. I then put each resulting file through demucs to get rid of any distortion caused by the different mastering of each track. Finally, I gave it some light dynamic processing to gain back some lost audio that was resulted from either the demucs extraction of the mastering difference distortion. There was also manual splitting to get back any lost vocal effects. Fair warning, I'm not 100% happy with "Princess Diana" and "Alone", but like I've said it's the best that it will ever be.

    Each file was stemmed from 44.1kHz/16-bit WAV files, and are now in FLAC [Level 8]. Have at it.
