    "Hero" is a song by British-Norwegian music producer and DJ Alan Walker featuring American singer-songwriter Sasha Alex Sloan, released on May 4 2023. The song is a perfect blend of Walker's signature cinematic melodies and undulating basslines with Sloan's powerful vocals. The lyrics of the song celebrate the unsung heroes who brighten up our lives every day, making it an emotive anthem that listeners can connect with.

    "Hero" is sure to be a hit this summer, and fans can expect to hear it during Walker's upcoming festival performances. The track follows his recent collaboration with NCS, "Dreamer," and is the second of many new releases planned for 2023.
