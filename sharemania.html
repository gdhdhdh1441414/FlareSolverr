    Blue Moon (stylized in all-caps; also known fully as JAY 1ST SOLO pt.2 [BLUE MOON]) is the first mini album by Jay. It was released on July 18, 2023 with "Blue Moon" serving as the album's title track.

    Track list
    1. "Blue Moon" - 2:51
    2. "Paradise" - 2:49
    3. "Magnet (자석)" (feat. Moon Sujin) - 3:15
    4. "Good Girls" - 3:05
    5. "Wait For You (데리러 갈게)" - 3:05
