    To Die For is the second full-length album by B.I. It was released on June 1, 2023 with "Die For Love" and "Dare To Love" serving as the album's double title tracks.



    It includes six out of the seven tracks from his 2021 EP Cosmos.

    The physical album comes in two versions: Dare to Love and Die for Love.

    Background
    On May 1, 2023, B.I announced his second full-length album To Die For, set to be released on June 1.[1]
