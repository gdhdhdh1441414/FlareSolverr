    The origin of this video:
    - I downloaded the entire iHeartRadio Music Festival 2015 Day 2 1080i HDTV 35 Mbps MPA2.0 H.264-TrollHD by TrollHD (88.7 GB!);
    - I extracted Tove's show using MKVtoolnix (lossless);
    - Using Avisynth, I deinterlaced the video with QTGMC for a smooth double fps (59.97 fps);
    - I converted the video to MP4 AVC CRF 18 Preset Slower using FFmpeg;
    - I converted the audio to WAV and I increased the volume with Audacity as much as possible without clipping;
    - I muxed the video and audio with Avidemux and I converted the audio to AAC 224 kbps;
    - I added chapters for each song with MKVtoolnix;
