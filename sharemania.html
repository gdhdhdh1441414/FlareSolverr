    Choice     was a short-lived R&B trio featuring Alecia Beth Moore, Sharon Flanagan, and Chrissy Conway. Moore would find later success as pop-rock superstar P!nk.

    While working on their debut album for LaFace Records, Moore was approached by label heads to consider a solo career instead. To date, only one song has been released officially -- "Key To My Heart," which was featured on the soundtrack to the movie Kazaam, starring Shaquille O'Neal.

    Two tracks from Choice's album, "Love Is Such A Crazy Thing" and "Let Me Let You Know," were reworked for P!nk's debut LP, 1999's Can't Take Me Home. Another album cut, "Stay," was re-recorded by Destiny's Child for their second album, The Writing's On the Wall.
