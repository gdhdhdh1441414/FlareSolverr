    https://www.discogs.com/release/2588619-Maroon-5-Hands-All-Over

    Track 25 is the Amazon exclusive track:

    https://www.amazon .com/Hands-Deluxe-Amazon-Exclusive-Version/dp/B005AACJZY

    Track 26-27 are the "Independent Record Store Exclusive":

    https://www.discogs.com/release/3701697-Maroon-5-Hands-All-Over

    Track 28 is an iTunes exclusive with an unvailable link:

    http://itunes.apple.com/ar/album/ha...321739&p=50217794&t=3884722&f=2240&u=13454574

    You can find track 28 on Spotify instead:

    https://open.spotify .com/track/5FRnvycJrLJxAfYr4aqx4Q

    Track 29 can be found on the Japanese edition:

    https://www.discogs.com/release/7932104-Maroon-5-Hands-All-Over

    Tracks 1-18 and 21 are 24-bit, 44.1kHz, and F.L.A.C. Tracks 19, 20, 22-27, and 29 are 16-bit, 44.kHz, F.L.A.C. Track 28 is 274kbps, 44.1kHz, and m4a. Please do not request a lossless version of track 28. It was publicly released in lossy format by the record label only.
