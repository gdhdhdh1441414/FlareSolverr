    Hosted on MEGA. May be unstable. If off, comment here! ;)

    Season 1
    - In "iDream of Dance", the opening credits are in blue, white and purple instead of pink and turquoise.

    Season 2
    - In "iPie", in the second-to-last clip, instead of using a scene from the episode itself (as used in the rest of the season), a scene from "iGo to Japan" is used, of the main trio being covered in mud.


    Season 3
    - From "iSpeed Date" onwards, a clip of Sam digging her thumb in an orange, from "iGo Nuclear", is replaced by Carly being hit by a pillow, coming from the episode "iCook";
    - From "iSaved Your Life" onward, the "GirlyCow" website in the browser bookmarks bar when typing "iCarly.com" is replaced by the "TheSlap" website from "Victorious" (Except for "iPsycho").


    Season 4
    - Only in "iGot a Hot Room", several clips of season 3 are used, being different from the rest of the fourth season. Also, the credits now are in 3D, and Noah Munck's name was added. Noah's graphics aren't in 3D yet;
    - The special episode "iParty With Victorious" has its own new intro.

    Season 5
    - "iLost My Mind" has a different intro from the rest of the fifth season. Also, this was the first intro that added blasting sound effects when the "iCarly" logo appears;
    - In "iStill Psycho", in the bookmarks bar, instead of showing "ZAPLOOK" it shows "HAPPY NEW YEAR", due to the episode being aired on December 31st, 2011.

    Season 6
    - The episode "iApril Fools" has an entirely special intro, with some clips and VFX being added to some parts and typing "iApril Fools" instead "iCarly.com" in the beginning. This intro also uses some clips of season 5;
    - The special "iShock America" also had its own intro, using some scenes of the episode itself, also typing "iShock America" instead of "iCarly.com" in the beginning;
    - Episodes between "iShock America" and "iGoodbye" had the "iShock America" intro reused, but typing "iCarly.com" as standard;
    - In "iRescue Carly", the intro is slightly more desaturated than the other episodes;
    - In the series finale, "iGoodbye", besides typing the episode title instead "iCarly.com" in the beginning, this intro has several clips from the previous seasons.
