    • Recorded in iZotope RX 7 Audio Editor at 96/24

    • Final export from iZotope RX 7 as 96/24 FLAC level 8
    • Tagged in kid3

    EQed files:
    • iZotope RX 7 as 96/24 FLAC level 8 files converted to 96/24 wav via MediaHuman Audio Converter
    • Converted files imported to Logic Pro
    • Newfangled Audio EQuivocate “match EQ” function used on Side A with Qobuz 48/24 digital release as input
    • EQ applied to sides A and B
    • Exported from Logic Pro as 96/24 wav
    • Reimported to iZotope RX 7 and exported as 96/24 FLAC level 8
