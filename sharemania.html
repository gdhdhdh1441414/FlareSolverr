    Brit Smith - Karma's A Bitch (The Jojostrumental Mix)
    • Format: WAV
    • Sample Rate: 24-Bit / 48 kHz
    I know I know - I am not the first person to think of this idea / do this mix / etc. This is not an original concept.
    However, after listening to a few other mixes they were either not full length duration to Brit's version (3:44) or their vocals were less than optimal (either had leftover drum pieces poking thru in the quiet parts at the beginning or vocals sounded very filtered).
    This mix I've made has the cleanest, clearest vocals, it is full length, and I've touched Brit's vocals up a bit to match the more modern production of Jojo / DJ White Shadow's version.
