    This Demonstration SACD Sounds Great with ANY Sennheiser or other Audiophile Headphones!

    Sennheiser Asia, the overseas branch of Sennheiser Germany, asked Stockfisch-Records to produce a sampler for them. The aim was to compile a test SACD for their new headphone model HD800: "for the optimal Sennheiser HD800 experience". The result is this sampler featuring recordings from Stockfish's top recording artists Steve Strauss, Sara K., Chris Jones and Christian Willisohn.

    Features:

    • SACD DSD64

    Selections:

    1. Mr. Bones - Steve Strauss

    2. Stars - Sara K.

    3. Turned My Upside Down - Sara K.

    4. Stop Those Bells (Live) - Sara K. & Chris Jones

    5. Caruso (Lucio Dalla) - Christian Willisohn

    Total playing time, 22:12. This Demonstration SACD Sounds Great with ANY Sennheiser or other Audiophile Headphones
