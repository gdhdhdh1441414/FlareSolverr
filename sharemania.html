    Metamorphosis is the second studio album by American actress and singer Hilary Duff. The album was released on August 26, 2003, by Hollywood Records and Buena Vista as the follow-up to her debut studio album and first Christmas album Santa Claus Lane (2002). According to Duff, the album incorporates elements of pop and rock music, and it represents changes that are specific to her life and that everyone experiences. Duff worked with several producers on the album such as The Matrix.[2] Others who collaborated on the album include Chico Bennett, Matthew Gerrard, John Shanks and Kara DioGuardi.

    Personal words:
    I love this album and it's a shame there wasn't anything special for its 20th anniversary, but come on, I took it upon myself to make a FAN MADE, I hope you enjoy it as much as I did, I spent hours creating and editing.
