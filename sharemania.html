    ISTJ is the third full-length album by NCT DREAM. It was released on July 17, 2023 with "ISTJ" serving as the album's title track.

    "Broken Melodies" was pre-released on June 19, 2023.

    The physical album comes in 24 versions: two Photobook versions (Introvert and Extrovert), Vending Machine, seven member-specific Poster versions, seven member-specific 7DREAM QR, and seven member-specific SMini.



    Track list
    "ISTJ" - 3:05
    "Broken Melodies" - 3:46
    "Yogurt Shake" - 3:17
    "Skateboard" - 3:06
    "Blue Wave (파랑)" - 3:10
    "Poison (모래성)" - 3:32
    "SOS" - 2:59
    "Pretzel (♡)" - 3:25

    "Like We Just Met" - 3:29
