    Recorded: ilya2129 from RuTracker
    Processed: me (caladan from sharemania.us)

    This is an awesome show by the very talented Norwegian artist Aurora. I got a video recording from RuTracker but it had a few issues (interlaced, 3 audio glitches in the midde of songs, audio slightly de-synced with the video and the volume too low, no chapters for songs), so I fixed them.

    Video:
    I deinterlaced the video using Avisynth QTGMC (the best deinterlacer) for a smooth double frame rate (59.94fps). I saved the video to MP4 AVC CRF 18 using FFmpeg. The video has enough bitrate to look fine on every display.

    Audio:
    I extracted the AC3 audio track. This track had 6 channels but only 2 were used. Using Audacity, I removed the empty channels, made the track stereo, raised the volume without clipping with the Amplify feature, surgically removed 3 broadcast glitches (the audio was noticeably silent for a faction of a second; these glitches are on all recordings I've seen of this show) and I added silence after the respective songs to maintain sync with the video. I saved the audio to AAC 256kbps CBR using dBPowerAmp.

    Chapters:
    I added chapters with titles for each song.

    Muxing:
    I used MKVmerge to put video, audio and chapters together in a MKV file. I synchronized audio and video tracks.

    Highly recommended recording for anyone with good taste in pop music. It has excellent quality and excellent setlist.
