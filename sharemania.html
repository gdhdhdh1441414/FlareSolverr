    Hello buddies,

    As some of you already might be aware, Google is now enforcing new rules and terms in regards to storage quota on Workspace accounts. I've already received the warning on the account.



    This is how it works:

    From 14 August onwards it will no longer be possible to upload any new files to share drives, so if you want to store more contents you have to do it during these next 2 month period. After this date all contents will remain read-only.

    All content will remain available for the next 2 years after this date, this meaning until 14 August, 2025.
