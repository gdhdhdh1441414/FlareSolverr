    In 2002, Celine Dion took part to a series of commercials for Chrysler Corporation. This one features Celine & her son Rene-Charles for the model Town &ountry on the song Have you ever been in love.

    Celine Dion The Voice Of Chrysler
    Rare 2003 Argentinean only 6-track promotional only enhanced CD to introduce Celine’s landmark car campaign for Chrysler Automobiles. Featuring all 6 of Celine’s Chrysler commercials as CD-ROM videos including the seldom seen 'Sebring' commercial. Presented in a Chrysler logo sleeve with full credits on the inside & back cover plus special insert explaining why Celine was chosen as 'The Voice of Chrysler' EPIC DEP764.

    Tracklist

    · “CROSSFIRE” Passion
    · “PACIFICA” Performance
    · “TOWN & COUNTRY” Family Values
