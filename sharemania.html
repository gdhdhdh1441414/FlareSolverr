    Genre: Electronic, Pop

    Year: 2019
    Video Source: Apple Music, thanks CAPRiCE24

    "Ghost" is a song produced by British-Norwegian music producer and DJ Alan Walker and performed by Ibiza-Born Antiguan-German singer-songwriter Au/Ra, released on October 17, 2019. It's the second track of Death Stranding: Timefall. Death Stranding: Timefall (Original Music from the World of Death Stranding) is a soundtrack album for the 2019 action game, Death Stranding. Featuring eight songs from various artists, it was released on November 7, 2019 in digital stores, through RCA Records and Sony Interactive Entertainment. It was included in all special editions of the game.

    The reason why the Japanese game developers trusted Alan Walker and Au/Ra to feature their special soundtrack is not something casual. The abilities from both artists are undeniable and this record is the ultimate proof of that. ‘Ghost‘ is a pure commercial trap track that perfectly showcases the producer and singer’s unique abilities that brought them to the main gates of the electronic music scene. Taking a more commercial approach and distancing himself from his classic future bass sounds, Alan Walker still finds a way to adapt his style delivering a very solid production. He creates a rhythmic and melodic bass filling it with a more tragic and alarming sound that becomes the perfect companion for the deep vocals from Au/Ra. Just as she did in other projects like ‘Panic Room‘ with CamelPhat, the Antiguan singer takes the lead role to impact the listener with her voice and set the tone of the song.

I met some of you backstage, some on stage, and others while you were out doing normal everyday stuff. I was moved by your reactions! I am excited to show you the process alongside the seriously talented Au/Ra."
