    Exist (stylized in all caps) is the seventh full-length album by EXO. It was released on July 10, 2023 with "Cream Soda" serving as the album's title track.

    "Let Me In" was pre-released on June 12, followed by "Hear Me Out" on June 30.

    The physical album comes in 19 versions: three photobook versions (E, X, and 0), eight member-specific digipack versions and eight member-specific SMini versions.
