    Cuts Both Ways is Gloria’s debut as a solo popstar, even though she became a solo artist, this album was produced by Miami Sound Machine too, she just wanted to be known more as herself and not part of a group. The album became an immediate huge success, selling more than 9 million physical copies all around the world, the album did well specially in the United States certifying triple platinum and selling more than 4 million copies just there, it topped the charts on a lot of big countries like Australia, Canada, Germany, Japan, United Kingdom, Mexico, Brazil and many more.

    This folder contains the full album (with latin spanish versions) + 2 bonus tracks in brazilian portuguese “Toda Pra Você (Here We Are)” & “Se Tenho Que Te Perder (Don’t Wanna Lose You)”[FLAC] from the brazilian release

    Only missing song in portuguese is “Amor Fatal (Your Love is Bad For Me)”

    it also contains one extended remix of “Oye mi Canto (English version)”
