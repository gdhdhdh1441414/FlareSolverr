    i'm searching for the DVD ISO of "Branca de Neve e os Sete Anões" (Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs) [Platinum Edition] released in Brazil in 2001, but can't find it anywhere.

    This version is rare, because it's the ONLY version that have the title and credits TRANSLATED to portuguese (br), and also, even the names of the dwarfs on their beds are translated (scene specially made for each language when the movie was made).

    Platinum Edition was the first release of the film in DVD, as you may know, and before, it was only available in VHS (that also had translated title and credits, etc). After that, Disney didn't included the translation on the international versions on the Diamond Edition and so on.
