    After months of recollecting remixes I had lost or needed in Lossless I am happy to say, I finally have pretty much everything I was looking for. I have made 2 albums for most of Kylie's singes CD 1 is the single and b-sides the second is the remixes. The first one I'm going to share is "2 Hearts" It has all but 2 known official remixes one being 2 Hearts (Alan Braxe Dub) the other the other "2 Hearts (Alan Braxe & Fred Falke Remix)" was only released on a 12" promo and I haven't found it in lossless. If anyone has those in lossless send them my way. It also includes the rare Cardiac Arrest Remix which was only available on a DJ service CD. Please hit like and say thank you, Enjoy! Dave
