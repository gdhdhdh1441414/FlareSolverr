    Youth in the Shade (stylized in all-caps) is the debut mini album by ZEROBASEONE. It was released on July 10, 2023 with "In Bloom" serving as the album's title track.

    The physical album comes in 11 versions: Youth, Shade, and nine member-specific digipack versions.




    Tracklist
    "Back to Zerobase" - 2:45
    "In Bloom" - 3:00
    "New Kidz On The Block" - 3:32
    "And I (우주먼지)" - 3:15
    "Our Season" - 2:57
    "Always" (Zhang Hao solo) - 2:09
