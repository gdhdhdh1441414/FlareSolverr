    I Feel (stylized as I feel) is the sixth mini album by (G)I-DLE. It was released on May 15, 2023 with "Queencard" serving as the album's title track.

    The physical release comes in twelve versions: Cat, Butterfly, Queen, individual member jewel case, Poca Album (Cat, Butterfly, Queen), and Photobook.





    Track list
    "Queencard (퀸카)" - 2:41
    "Allergy" - 2:42
    "Lucid" - 2:55
    "All Night" - 2:26
    "Paradise" - 3:09
    "Peter Pan (어린 어른)" - 3:10
