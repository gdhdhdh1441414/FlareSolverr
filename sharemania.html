    "One More Time" is the debut single album by BB GIRLS. It was released on August 3, 2023 with "One More Time" serving as the single's title track.
    This marks their first comeback since their departure from Brave Entertainment and their first after their name change from Brave Girls to BB Girls under their new agency.



    Track list
    1. "One More Time" - 3:07
    2. "Lemonade" - 3:00
