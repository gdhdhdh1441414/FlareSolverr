    This is Taylor Swift's famous Sydney 2015 show broadcasted by Apple, one of my favorite concert recordings of all time (definitely in top 10). I separated the actual performance from the behind the scenes clips inserted through the entire show. The best part: the editing has been done losslessly with MKV ToolNix (so the quality is identical as before). It took me so much time to pick the right I-frames to do the cutting. Literally inspecting some sections frame by frame.

    I made chapters for each song and I included a cover image + info file with the setlist. This video has subtitles (including lyrics for each song) and 2 audio tracks: stereo and Dolby Digital 5.1.

    Bonus: audio bootleg!

    I included the audio version of this show. It's in m4a format from the AAC track of the video. I split the AAC losslessly with FFcuesplitter based on the chapter file that was converted to CUE with chapterEditor, moved AAC to M4A containers with ffmpeg, added tags with Mp3Tag, made a cover with Photoshop.

    Enjoy!
