    “Highway” is a song by American electronic music DJ trio Cheat Codes (comprised of Trevor Dahl, Matthew Russell and Kevin Ford), featuring Mexican singer and songwriter Sofía Reyes. It is released as a single on Aug 23, 2019.

    “They say life is a highway and we all travel our own roads, some good some bad, yet each is a blessing in itself,” Cheat Codes’ Kevi Ford said about the meaning of the track.

    There is also a version with a verse in Spanish sung by French DJ, record producer and singer Willy William. The version of this release is without the Spanish verse.

    Release Info
    The video track is upscaled to 4K with Topaz Video AI and encoded to HEVC 10-bit.
    The PCM track is lossless (24-bit 48 kHz) and is the default track from the master.
    The timed lyrics are synced & edited. Enable the subtitles to see the lyrics.
