    Kylie Minogue - Bonfire Heart [Unreleased 2017 Album] (320kbps mp3)

    For those of you feeling overwhelmed by the leaks from a few weeks back, I’ve put together a conceptual unreleased album using the best of the 50+ leaked tracks that fit the theme of something we’ve never really gotten from Kylie… a proper breakup album. I imagine if Golden had never happened this is the album we could have gotten in 2017. It’s titled Bonfire Heart after a lyric from opening track (and what I image to be the lead single) “Rewind”. I’ve also used the Tony Hung designed logo from the aborted 2016 album project (that was ultimately only used on merch for the 2016 RAH Christmas show). The tracks have been slightly edited for uniformity and some starts/endings have been edited to be a bit cleaner. I hope you all enjoy it as much as I’ve been!




    Kylie Minogue - Bonfire Heart

    1. Rewind
    2. Sad Songs
    3. Beautiful Disaster
    4. Like Lightning
    5. What Do You Know
    6. The Loneliest Kind of Lover
    7. Four Letter Word (feat. Jack Savoretti)
    8. Uncut Stone
    9. Shooting Star
    10. Was It Ever Really Love?
    11. Tears of Joy
