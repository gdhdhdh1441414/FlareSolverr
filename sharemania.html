    AOA (에이오에이; an acronym for Ace of Angels) is a girl group under FNC Entertainment. They debuted on August 9, 2012 with the single album "Angels' Story".
    The group originally consisted of eight members; Cho A, Ji Min, Yu Na, You Kyung, Min A, Hye Jeong, Seol Hyun and Chan Mi, who promote alternately as a group and as a band. Until 2013, when the band debuted as the first official sub-unit, AOA Black, consisting of Cho A, Ji Min, Yu Na, You Kyung and Min A, with the digital single "Moya" and they started to promote only as group.
    In 2016, their second official sub-unit called AOA Cream, consisting of Yu Na, Hye Jeong and Chan Mi, debuted with the digital single "I'm Jelly Baby".
